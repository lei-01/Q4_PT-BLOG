MANIFESTO IN MANILA

February 3-5, 2023

Manifesto in Manila felt like a dream finally coming true. It was ENHYPEN’s first concert in the Philippines, and the energy inside the arena was unforgettable—fans singing every lyric, light sticks glowing, and emotions running high. It wasn’t just a show; it was a special moment that brought ENHYPEN and Filo ENGENEs closer, creating memories we will cherish forever.

FATE IN NEW CLARK CITY

February 2, 2024

FATE in New Clark City was truly unforgettable—it wasn’t just another concert, it was history in the making. Seeing ENHYPEN perform in a massive stadium for the first time in the Philippines felt surreal, especially knowing we were the final stop of their world tour. The energy of around 25,000 fans, the fireworks, and the powerful performances made the night magical. More than anything, it showed how strong the bond is between ENHYPEN and Filo ENGENEs—a night filled with love, music, and memories we’ll never forget.

WALK THE LINE IN BULACAN

March 1, 2025

Walk the Line in Bulacan felt like a night we had all been waiting for. Held on March 1, 2025 at the Philippine Sports Stadium, the concert brought thousands of fans together for powerful performances, stunning visuals, and nonstop energy. Being the group’s first stop for their Asia tour made it even more special, and hearing the members express their love for the Philippines reminded us how strong our connection is—turning the entire night into a memory we will never forget.
